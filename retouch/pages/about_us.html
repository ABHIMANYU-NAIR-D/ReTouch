About ReTouch

Transforming plastic waste into accessible education, one page at a time. Discover our journey, meet our team, and learn about our mission to create a sustainable future.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the publishing industry by transforming plastic waste into affordable, high-quality books, with a special focus on creating accessible Braille editions for visually impaired readers. We believe education should be both sustainable and inclusive.

Our Vision

A world where environmental sustainability and educational accessibility go hand in hand. We envision a future where every piece of plastic waste becomes a gateway to knowledge, and every person has access to affordable, quality educational materials.

Our Journey

From a simple idea to a movement that's changing lives and protecting our planet

January 2022

The Spark of an Idea

Founder Priya Sharma witnessed the environmental impact of plastic waste during a beach cleanup in Mumbai. The idea of transforming this waste into something valuable for education was born.

June 2022

First Prototype

After months of research and experimentation, we successfully created our first recycled sheet from plastic bottles. The quality exceeded expectations, proving the concept was viable.

December 2022

First Braille Book

We printed our first Braille book using recycled sheets, partnering with the National Association for the Blind. This milestone marked our commitment to accessibility.

July 2024

Major Milestone

Reached 50,000 plastic bottles recycled and 12,500 books created. Expanded operations to three cities and established partnerships with 25 educational institutions.

Our Technology

Advanced processes and technologies that make our mission possible

Our proprietary plastic processing system uses advanced thermal depolymerization to break down PET bottles into base polymers. The process operates at optimized temperatures to ensure minimal energy consumption while maintaining material integrity. Quality control sensors monitor the entire process to ensure consistent output.

The processed plastic is reformed into high-quality sheets using precision molding technology. Our calendering process ensures uniform thickness and smooth surface finish. Each sheet undergoes rigorous testing for durability, printability, and tactile properties essential for Braille applications.

Our specialized Braille embossing system creates precise tactile dots with consistent height and spacing. The technology accommodates both single and double-sided Braille printing while maintaining the structural integrity of our recycled sheets. Advanced software ensures accurate translation and formatting.

Comprehensive quality control includes automated optical inspection, tactile verification systems, and durability testing. Each batch undergoes environmental stress testing to ensure longevity. Real-time monitoring systems track production metrics and maintain consistent quality standards across all products.
