Our 6-Step Process

Each step is carefully designed to maximize environmental impact while ensuring the highest quality output

1

Plastic Collection

We partner with local communities, schools, and organizations to collect plastic waste, focusing on PET bottles and containers.

Collection Methods

  • Community drop-off points
  • School collection drives
  • Corporate partnerships

Quality Standards

  • Clean, label-free bottles
  • PET plastic only
  • No contamination
2

Sorting & Cleaning

Collected plastic undergoes rigorous sorting by type and color, followed by thorough cleaning to remove all contaminants.

Sorting Process

  • Automated optical sorting
  • Color separation
  • Quality inspection

Cleaning Methods

  • Hot water washing
  • Chemical treatment
  • Final rinse & dry
3

Mechanical Shredding

Clean plastic bottles are mechanically shredded into small, uniform flakes that will serve as raw material for the next stage.

Shredding Specifications

2-5mm
Flake Size

4

Melting & Extrusion

Plastic flakes are heated to precise temperatures and extruded into thin sheets with the perfect texture for printing.

Temperature Control

Melting Point 260°C
Extrusion Temp 280°C
Cooling Rate 15°C/min

Sheet Properties

  • 0.1mm thickness
  • Smooth surface finish
  • Print-ready quality
5

Digital Printing

Our recycled sheets are printed using eco-friendly inks and specialized equipment for both regular text and Braille formats.

Printing Technologies

  • Digital offset printing
  • Braille embossing
  • Color illustration printing

Eco-Friendly Inks

  • Soy-based inks
  • VOC-free formulation
  • Biodegradable components
6

Binding & Distribution

Printed pages are bound into durable books and distributed to schools, libraries, and individuals who need them most.

Binding Options

  • Perfect binding
  • Spiral binding
  • Hardcover options

Distribution Channels

  • Schools & libraries
  • NGO partnerships
  • Direct delivery
